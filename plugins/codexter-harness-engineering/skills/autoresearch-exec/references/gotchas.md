# Autoresearch Exec Gotchas

- Never stage with `git add -A`.
- Never keep an improvement with failing guard checks.
- Do not rerun the same failed hypothesis without a new reason.
- Do not summarize every iteration in chat; JSONL is the memory.
- Do not use destructive reset unless revert cannot complete and user work is
  protected.
- Do not let `autoresearch.md` drift behind major learnings.
