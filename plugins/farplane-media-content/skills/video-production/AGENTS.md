# Video Production Skill Instructions

This module owns domain video workflows and method routing. Keep the first-load
`SKILL.md` focused on method selection, artifact structure, prompt/script
planning, source-asset boundaries, and handoff to execution skills.

Do not duplicate model maps, `belt` commands, Remotion authoring rules, or
render schemas here. Route those to `video-generation`, `remotion`,
`remotion-render`, or `image-generation`.

Use one primary method per task. Add a supporting method only when the requested
deliverable genuinely crosses method boundaries, such as a campaign video that
also needs platform paid-social specs.

Preserve upstream/prompting references under method-specific filenames so this
skill can keep one public surface without flattening useful domain detail.

Keep external spend, uploads, publishing, ad buying, and likeness approval
boundaries explicit.
