# Research Synthesis Gate

Use this before landing-page story, asset, and layout planning. The goal is not
to copy competitors; it is to find the real buyer expectations a strong page
must satisfy, then choose one differentiated creative take.

## Source Set

Pick 3-5 sources:

- 2-3 direct competitors or peer products.
- 1 category leader or inspiration site with the desired quality bar.
- 1 operational, regulatory, or buyer-context source when claims are sensitive.

For live websites, inspect current pages or screenshots. Do not rely on memory
for unstable competitor positioning, claims, metrics, or product imagery.

## Extract

For each source, record:

- `asset strategy`: real product, real customer/workplace, generated scene,
  video, frame sequence, UI screenshot, abstract render, iconography.
- `storyline`: hero promise, problem, mechanism, proof, CTA.
- `layout moves`: full-bleed media, split panels, sticky sections, comparison
  tables, proof bands, quote blocks, product cards.
- `motion`: scroll scrub, video autoplay, reveal, 3D/WebGL, static.
- `proof`: metrics, named customers, regulatory language, citations, awards,
  testimonials, disclaimers.
- `claim boundary`: what the source is careful not to claim.

## Best-of-worlds

Route the extracted patterns through `best-of-worlds`:

| Pattern | Source | Evidence | Decision | Local use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

Use `adopt` only for patterns with strong fit and low risk. Use `adapt` for
strong principles that need local copy, proof, or asset changes. Use `reject`
for attractive patterns that would weaken trust or overclaim. Use `defer` for
patterns that need assets, customers, or claims the run does not have.

## Brainstorm Unique Take

After the decision matrix, use `brainstorm` to choose 2-4 possible creative
angles and recommend one:

- What is the unexpected narrative frame?
- What visual world makes this page memorable?
- What proof ladder makes it believable?
- What should this page refuse to become?

## Spec Requirement

The approved `LANDING_SPEC.md` must include:

- `Reference Research`
- `Best-of-worlds Decisions`
- `Unique Take`

If any of these are missing, the executor should return to Planner instead of
building.
