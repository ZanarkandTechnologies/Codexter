# Marketing Video Prompting

Use this for product launch, promo, testimonial, brand story, feature highlight, and social marketing video prompts.

## Prompt Shape

```text
[marketing role] + [product/offer] + [single viewer payoff] + [scene/action] + [camera] + [lighting/style] + [brand tone] + [platform/aspect]
```

## Artifact-Specific Rules

- Start from the campaign job: launch, feature highlight, testimonial, before/after, brand story, UGC ad, or product demo.
- Put the product or offer in the first clause so the generated clip does not become generic lifestyle footage.
- Express one payoff per clip. If the ad needs three benefits, make three shots.
- Include commercial intent: product reveal, problem moment, transformation, customer reaction, feature close-up, or CTA slate.
- Add platform constraints when relevant: `vertical 9:16`, `square 1:1`, `works without sound`, `space for text overlay`, or `hook in first 2 seconds`.

## Templates

```text
Product reveal:
"Premium product launch video for [product], [product] emerging from [setting], slow push-in camera, dramatic controlled lighting, clean commercial style, [brand adjectives], [aspect ratio]"

Problem/solution:
"Relatable [audience] struggling with [problem], quick cut to [product] solving it, visible before/after transformation, authentic commercial style, [platform format]"

Testimonial:
"Customer testimonial style shot of [persona] speaking to camera about [benefit], natural office/lifestyle background, warm professional lighting, authentic and credible, space for captions"
```

## Avoid

- Generic phrases like `cool marketing video` without product, audience, or payoff.
- Multiple unrelated benefits in one shot.
- Over-polished UGC prompts when the platform needs native/authentic social style.
