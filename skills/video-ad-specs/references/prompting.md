# Video Ad Prompting

Use this for paid social ads, platform-specific hooks, short-form creative, UGC-style ads, bumper ads, and caption-safe scenes.

## Prompt Shape

```text
[platform/format] + [hook] + [audience/problem] + [product/action] + [visual proof] + [camera/editing style] + [safe-zone/text constraints] + [CTA]
```

## Artifact-Specific Rules

- Prompt the platform first: TikTok/Reels/Shorts vertical, YouTube bumper 16:9, Facebook feed 1:1 or 4:5, LinkedIn professional.
- Design the first second as the hook for short-form ads.
- Make the prompt sound native to the platform: UGC/authentic for TikTok, clear commercial for YouTube, professional proof for LinkedIn.
- Leave safe zones and text space when captions, CTA, or UI overlays will be added later.
- Use separate prompts for hook, proof, offer, and CTA when the ad needs multiple beats.

## Templates

```text
TikTok/Reels:
"Vertical 9:16 UGC-style ad, first-second hook showing [problem/curiosity], [persona] uses [product] to get [result], handheld natural camera, bright authentic room, caption-safe center framing, native social style"

YouTube bumper:
"6-second 16:9 bumper ad for [product], immediate product reveal, one clear benefit, fast polished cuts, strong final logo/CTA space, high-energy commercial style"

LinkedIn:
"Professional B2B video ad for [product], [persona] discovering [business result], clean office setting, readable dashboard area, calm confident camera movement, caption-friendly composition"
```

## Avoid

- Reusing the same visual prompt across platforms without aspect ratio, safe-zone, and native-style changes.
- Slow cinematic openings for short-form ads.
- Exact text inside generated footage when captions/overlays should own the wording.
